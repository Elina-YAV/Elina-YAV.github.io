Weekly Chats

Information for Weekly Chats

Elina or a special speaker will lead the chats.

Each session will start by reading Young Able Voices preamble that will list the expectations and rules followed by a fun check in question and anything else people might want to share.

Parents will be sent an email if we feel they should be informed.

Self harm or harming others won't be discussed

Expectations for Weekly Chats

Be kind

Be respectful

Be assertive

Respect the privacy of everyone in the group.
