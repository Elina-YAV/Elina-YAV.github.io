A safe place for kids with hidden disabilities to connect, and know they aren't alone.

Your future is bright.☀️

At Young Able Voices, children with hidden disabilities will have the opportunity to join weekly chats to talk with others facing similar challenges that relate to their disabilities. Of course, if you feel a little shy, no problem, you can certainly just join the chat and listen in as well. When you feel like you would like to share and speak to the group, you are always welcome to.

Each week we will have a specific video posted to get the chat rolling. This video will be posted a few days prior to each chat and will give you an idea of where the conversation will begin. Of course, we never can predict where it will end.

Please understand that this is a peer-to-peer support group, and we are all just looking to give out helpful suggestions, tips, and tricks in navigating daily life as a kid facing challenges. We are not therapists, nor do we pretend to be. Rather, we are a peer support group.

Hey Moms, Dads, and all you awesome adults—feel free to join your kiddos and tune in! Got questions or topic ideas for our chats? Shoot me an email—I'd love to hear from you!

I can be reached at elina@youngablevoices.com. Please do understand that I am a college student with a very active calendar and may not be able to reply each day, but will do my best within 48 - 72 hours.
